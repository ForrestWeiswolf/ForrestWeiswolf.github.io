I'm a songwriter, play the bowed psaltery, and sing frequently even though I never got to be much good at it.

Every year, I participate in February Album Writing Month (FAWM), a challenge to write and record an album during the month of February. Most of these aren't currently available, as mixing and arranging is a much longer process than songwriting; but links to a few songs that are further along in that process or otherwise noteworthy can be found on this page.

A few of the projects I've worked on can be found below.

Echo

When practcing a song, I sometimes want to play and record a very short part of it, listen to that in order to hear whether I got the timing right, then repeat, potentially repeating this many times in a row.

Putting down my bow to reset the recording every time is inconvenient and distracting when working on a section that's only seconds long, so I made this webpage, which records for a specified number of seconds, plays it back, then waits a specified number of seconds before starting over.

ForrestWeiswolf.github.io/echo/
