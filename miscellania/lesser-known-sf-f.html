--- layout: default title: Favorite lesser-known SF&F ---

At some point, I made a list of 'Favorite SF&F that someone who likes many of the same books as me might not have heard of'.

  • Dimension of Miracles, by Robert Sheckley
  • Blue and Gold, by K. J. Parker
  • Invisible Cities, by Italo Calvino
  • The Collapsium, by Wil McCarthy
  • A Madness of Angels, by Kate Griffin
  • Every Heart A Doorway, by Seanan McGuire
  • Radiance, by Cathrynne Valente
